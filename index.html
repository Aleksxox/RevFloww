FAQ

Common questions

Still got questions? Email us at hello@revflow.io — we reply within a few hours.

Is there really no card required for the trial?
+
Correct — no card, no payment details, nothing. Sign up with your email and you get 3 full days of access. At the end of the trial we'll ask you to choose a plan and add payment details if you want to continue. If you don't, your account simply pauses. No charges, no surprises.
Can I cancel before being charged?
+
Yes, any time during or after your trial. Go to account settings and cancel in one click. No emails, no phone calls, no friction. Cancel before the trial ends and you will never be charged a penny.
How do I enter my data into the dashboard?
+
You enter your numbers directly inside the REVFLOW dashboard — no spreadsheets needed. Each metric has its own input field. Type in your daily dials, replies, bookings, and closes and everything calculates instantly. Your data saves automatically. You can also export any day's data as a CSV in one click from the dashboard header. If you use Instagram for outreach, copying your DM counts across takes about 90 seconds a day.
Can my whole sales team use it?
+
Pro includes 3 seats, Agency is unlimited. Each person gets their own secure login and individual metrics view. Managers see the combined team leaderboard on the Command Centre tab.
Is my data safe?
+
All data is encrypted in transit over HTTPS. User accounts are powered by Supabase — enterprise-grade authentication. We never sell or share your data with anyone, ever.
Can I use REVFLOW for my clients?
+
Yes — the Agency plan is built exactly for this. Each client gets their own sub-account and login. You manage everything from one admin view. White-label options available on request.
